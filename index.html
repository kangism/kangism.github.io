    Short Bio


    Wei KANG is currently a research fellow and research degree supervisor at the University of South Australia (UniSA) and research engineer at the Data to Decisions Cooperative Research Centre (D2D CRC). He obtained his PhD in Computer Science from the National University of Singapore in 2015.

    For more details, please visit https://www.linkedin.com/in/wkang.
